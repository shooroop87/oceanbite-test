About Oceanbite Seafood

Oceanbite Seafood was born from a passion for exceptional seafood and a commitment to sustainable sourcing. Our story began in the heart of the European Union, where we built strong relationships with trusted fisheries and developed deep expertise in the wild-caught seafood industry. With a focus on quality, traceability, and reliability, we quickly established a reputation for excellence among European partners.

As demand for premium seafood grew, so did we. Oceanbite has since expanded across the Atlantic and is now proudly headquartered in California, where we continue to serve the needs of businesses across the U.S. Our mission is to bridge the gap between Europe's rich fishing traditions and the growing appetite for superior-quality seafood in North America.

What We Do

We specialize in salmon and trout—sourced directly from EU-certified fisheries—and offer a carefully curated selection of other high-grade seafood products. Every item we deliver meets rigorous standards for freshness, flavor, and sustainability.

At Oceanbite, we understand the unique demands of B2B clients, from restaurants and wholesalers to food service providers and retailers. That's why we provide tailored supply solutions, flexible order volumes, and full white-label services for brands looking to offer premium seafood under their own name.

More than just a supplier, we aim to be your strategic partner—delivering consistent quality.

Our deep-rooted relationships with EU fisheries ensure consistent access to top-quality, wild-caught seafood that meets the highest standards of taste, safety, and sustainability. Every product we deliver is carefully selected and handled with the utmost care.

Whether you require specific cuts, bulk orders, or flexible delivery schedules, we're here to support your business growth with premium seafood solutions that your customers will love.
