Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our seafood products and services.

General Questions

What types of seafood do you supply?

We specialize in premium wild-caught salmon, trout, and caviar from European Union waters. Our products include whole fish, fillets, portions, smoked varieties, and premium caviar selections.

Where do you source your seafood?

All our seafood is sourced from certified fisheries within the European Union that adhere to strict sustainability and quality standards. We maintain direct relationships with trusted suppliers to ensure premium quality and traceability.

Do you offer white-label services?

Yes, we provide white-label services for businesses looking to expand their product lines with premium seafood under their own brand name. Contact us to discuss custom packaging and branding options.

Ordering & Delivery

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product type. For whole fish and bulk orders, we typically require minimum quantities of 100-500 lbs. For specialty items like caviar, minimums may be lower. Contact us for specific product minimums.

How quickly can you fulfill orders?

Standard orders are typically fulfilled within 5-7 business days from our California facility. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee. Delivery times depend on your location and shipping method selected.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes, we ship to businesses across the United States. All seafood is shipped frozen with appropriate cold chain packaging to maintain quality and freshness during transport.

Quality & Certification

What quality certifications do you have?

Our products meet HACCP standards and are sourced from EU-certified fisheries. We maintain full traceability documentation and can provide certificates of origin and quality analysis upon request.

How do you ensure freshness during shipping?

All products are flash-frozen at source and maintained in a continuous cold chain. We use specialized insulated packaging with dry ice or gel packs to ensure products arrive at optimal temperature.

Sustainability

Are your fishing practices sustainable?

Absolutely. We partner exclusively with fisheries that use selective fishing methods, minimize bycatch, and operate under strict EU sustainability regulations. All our wild-caught seafood comes from well-managed, certified fisheries.

Can you provide traceability information?

Yes, every product can be traced back to its specific source fishery, including vessel information, catch date, and processing facility. We provide complete supply chain transparency upon request.

Pricing & Payment

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we offer competitive pricing for larger volume orders. Our sales team can provide custom quotes based on your specific needs and order frequency.

What payment terms do you accept?

We offer flexible payment terms for established business customers, including net 30 terms for qualified accounts. New customers may require payment on delivery or advance payment for initial orders.
