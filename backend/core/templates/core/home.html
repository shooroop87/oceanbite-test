About

Oceanbite Seafood began its journey in the European Union, rooted in a deep tradition of sourcing premium wild-caught seafood. Today, we've grown into a California-based business, proudly delivering superior quality seafood to partners across the U.S. With a focus on excellence, sustainability, and reliability, we bring the best of European waters to your business.

From European Waters to Your Business—Tailored, Trusted, and Always Fresh

We specialize in delivering premium-grade salmon and trout directly from the European Union to your business—fast, fresh, and fully customized. Our deep-rooted relationships with EU fisheries ensure consistent access to top-quality, wild-caught seafood that meets the highest standards of taste, safety, and sustainability.

We understand that every business has unique needs. That's why we offer tailored solutions—whether you require specific cuts, bulk orders, or flexible delivery schedules. For brands looking to expand their product line, we also provide white-label services, allowing you to offer world-class seafood under your own brand name.
