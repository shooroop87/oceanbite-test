Sustainability

Committed to Quality. Rooted in Responsibility

At Oceanbite Seafood, we believe that offering premium seafood goes hand in hand with protecting the oceans it comes from. Our promise is simple: to provide your business with the highest quality wild-caught seafood—while upholding the values of sustainability, traceability, and ethical sourcing.

We work exclusively with trusted fisheries across the European Union that follow strict environmental and fishing regulations. By sourcing responsibly, we help preserve fish populations, protect marine ecosystems, and support the long-term health of the seafood industry.

From catch to delivery, we ensure that every step of our supply chain is transparent, efficient, and environmentally conscious. We also continually assess and improve our practices to meet the evolving standards of global sustainability—because delivering great seafood should never come at the cost of the planet.

Whether you're sourcing for a high-end restaurant or a growing brand, you can count on Oceanbite to deliver with integrity, consistency, and care.

Certifications

We proudly partner with certified suppliers and maintain compliance with internationally recognized standards to guarantee quality, safety, and sustainability. Our products are sourced and handled in accordance with:

MSC (Marine Stewardship Council)

for sustainably caught wild seafood

HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points)

ensuring food safety throughout our supply chain

EU Catch Certification Scheme

ensuring full traceability and legal origin

FDA Compliance

for all seafood imported into the United States

We are committed to continuous improvement and regularly audit our operations and partners to uphold these certifications and exceed industry expectations.
